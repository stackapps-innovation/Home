Who We Are

StackApps - Your Premium IT Partner

StackApps is a Indian technology company that creates web, mobile, and computer software for its partners in the IT industry. In collaboration with Envato, the world's largest software marketplace, they are selling software. StackApps is one of codecanyon's fastest-growing software providers.

In 2023, Thomas Selvanathan, Aadhinarayanan Chelliah and Dhanasekaran Ramasamy founded StackApps to develop and sell software licenses to entrepreneurs and start-ups. Their primary goal was to provide low-cost software to entrepreneurs.
