Top Features Of Admin & Store Panel
All on one Powerful Complete Multivendor Online Food Delivery Script
Complete Featured User App

With this bundle, you will receive the source code for the StackCommerce mobile app and will be able to expand your business. We created this with flutter so that your app can be used on both Android and iOS.

Multiple Payment Gateway

This e-commerce content management system includes a number of payment gateways. They are all well-known, and you can get started right away. If you cannot find your preferred payment gateway, please contact us and we will integrate it for you.

Push Notification

Firebase order status push notifications and marketing purpose push notifications increase app engagement. Your customers may add products to their wishlist but forget to check out, so you can send push notifications to remind them. It will also assist you in increasing your sales.

Chat, Coupon, Tracking & Review Pro Features

There are dozens of interesting features already integrated with StackCommerce cms; simply browse our demos to learn more. You can get live chat options, coupons, tracking, reviews, and a plethora of other amazing features that will assist you in running small to medium-sized businesses that sell their products online.

POS System

In the admin panel, you will find an integrated POS system. The order details, status, and customer information are all available.

Order Management

You will receive all order details with a single click. There are order categories (pending, confirmed, returned, delivered, and so on) to help you manage your orders.

Product Management

You can manage multiple product brands and place them in various categories. There will also be sections for in-house and seller products. You can display your favorite products and brands in the special section of the home page.

Marketing Section

This is an all-inclusive online store solution. It will provide you with a feature-rich admin panel from which you can manage all aspects of primary marketing. Banners, coupons, push notifications, flash sales, and featured sales can help your business grow quickly.

Statistics and Reporting

You'll get a visual report of your company's cash flow and financial status, complete with statistics and reports. You will receive an earnings report, an order report, and a sales report.

Employee Management

If you have a problem with employee management, you can solve it by using the employee section. You can manage employee roles and create a list of new hires.

Delivery Man Management

By making a detailed list, you can hire an infinite number of delivery men. You can always add a new delivery man to the list.

Multi vendor e-commerce system

SackCommerce is a multi-vendor ecommerce solution that includes user and vendor mobile apps. You can also disable multi-vendor functionality and use ecommerce as a single vendor.

Option For Multiple Shipping Methods

Multiple shipping options enable you to include your preferred shipping method, which users can choose as their preferred method.

Top-notch E-commerce CMS System To Boost Your Business Growth

Your business will stand out with a unique shop design. All pages and attributes are adequately created for every device size, including desktop, laptop, mobile, tablet, and other mobile devices.

Top-Level Support

After you acquire the script from Codecanyon, 6amTech is ready to assist you in any way. We also have a lot of paperwork, videos, and tutorials.

Multilingual Option

This system supports multiple languages; all you have to do is enter your translations in the admin area.

100% Responsive

We proudly make this E-commerce CMS system with 100% web and admin panels entirely responsive. We want to take part in your successful online business journey, so we develop it with quality in mind.

Both iOS and Android Apps (Flutter)

We built the admin, user, and delivery app in the Flutter-cross platform with extensive UI\UX design. So, the app is very user-friendly. For both web and apps, it will give you a seamless experience.
